How to play

  • You start with a board with words on it.
  • Everyone will get the location of a word on the board... except for one person.
  • After that, take turns saying a related word without giving away the secret word.
  • For example, if the word is apple you might say "red".
  • Even the person who doesn't know the word has to say a related word. Try not to get caught!
  • At the end, you guess who doesn't know the word.
