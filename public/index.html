How to play

3-12 players

  • Start with words in a chart.
  • Everyone except one person receives a secret word.
  • Take turns saying a word related to the secret word.
  • Don't give away the secret word!
  • For example, if the word is apple you might say "red".
  • Even the person who doesn't know the word has to say a related word. Try not to get caught!
  • At the end, everyone guesses who doesn't know the word.
  • If you get caught not knowing the word, you get a chance to guess it.
